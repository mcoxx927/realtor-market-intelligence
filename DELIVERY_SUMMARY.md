## GENERATION METHODOLOGY

### Health Score Calculation (0-100)
Starting at 50 (neutral), adjusted by:

**Positive Factors:**
- Pending ratio >0.5: +10 points
- Days on market <45: +15 points
- New listings growing: +5 points
- Prices rising: +10 points

**Negative Factors:**
- Price reductions >50% of inventory: -15 points
- Days on market growing >20% YoY: -10 points
- Prices declining YoY: -5 to -15 points

**Result Range:**
- 70-100: BULLISH (seller's market)
- 45-69: NEUTRAL (balanced market)
- 0-44: BEARISH (buyer's market)

### Sentiment Determination
Based on health score thresholds above, providing clear market characterization.

### Alert Triggers
**HIGH SEVERITY:**
- Price drop >3% MoM
- Price reductions +>30% MoM
- New listings drop >20% MoM
- Pending ratio <0.35

**MEDIUM SEVERITY:**
- DOM increase >20% MoM
- Inventory increase >25% MoM
- Price increases drop >30% MoM
- Any metric >30% worse YoY

**LOW SEVERITY:**
- New listings change >15% MoM
- Pending ratio change >10% MoM
- DOM change >15% from 3-month avg
